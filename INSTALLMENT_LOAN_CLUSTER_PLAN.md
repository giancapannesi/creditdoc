## Content Plan — 12 Pieces

### Money Pages (listicles — highest conversion)
1. **`/best/installment-loans/`** — "Best Installment Loan Companies [2026]: Rates, Terms & Reviews"
   - Links to 15-20 top installment lenders from our directory
   - Comparison table: APR range, loan amounts, terms, credit score needed
   - Internal links to state law pages, vs-comparison posts

2. **`/best/installment-loans-bad-credit/`** — "Best Installment Loans for Bad Credit [2026]"
   - Links to lenders that accept 500-600 credit scores
   - Consumer protection warnings about predatory terms
   - Links to credit repair resources, state complaint pages

3. **`/best/online-installment-loans/`** — "Best Online Installment Loans [2026]: Apply From Home"
   - Focus on lenders with online applications
   - Speed of funding, approval process comparison

### Pillar Content (informational — SEO traffic magnets)
4. **Blog: "What Is an Installment Loan? How It Works, Types & What to Watch For"**
   - Define installment loan, how payments work, amortization
   - Types: personal, auto, mortgage, student
   - Pros/cons, when to use one
   - Links to: /best/ listicles, calculator, state pages

5. **Blog: "Installment Loans vs Payday Loans: Which Is Safer for Borrowers?"**
   - Head-to-head comparison table
   - APR comparison (36% vs 400%+), term length, rollover risk
   - Consumer protection angle — CreditDoc's strength
   - Links to: payday loan state laws, lender profiles

6. **Blog: "Types of Installment Loans Explained: Secured vs Unsecured, Short vs Long Term"**
   - Covers: personal, auto, mortgage, student, buy-now-pay-later
   - Decision tree: which type for which situation
   - Links to relevant lender categories

7. **Blog: "Installment Loans for Bad Credit: What Are Your Real Options?"**
   - Honest assessment of options at each credit tier (500, 550, 600, 650)
   - Warning about "guaranteed approval" scams
   - Links to: /best/installment-loans-bad-credit/, credit repair resources

### Supporting Content (long-tail captures)
8. **Blog: "How to Compare Installment Loan Offers (APR, Fees, Prepayment Penalties)"**
   - Teaches consumers what to look for
   - Links to: calculator, lender profiles with pricing data

9. **Blog: "Installment Loan vs Revolving Credit: What's the Difference?"**
   - Explains credit types, how each affects credit score
   - Links to: credit-building resources

10. **Blog: "How Many Installment Loans Can You Have at Once?"**
    - Answers common question with state-by-state context
    - Links to: state legislation pages

11. **Blog: "Installment Loan Requirements: Credit Score, Income & What Lenders Look For"**
    - What you need to qualify
    - How to improve chances of approval
    - Links to: lender profiles, credit repair

12. **Blog: "Are Installment Loans Good for Your Credit Score?"**
    - How installment loans appear on credit reports

    - Links to: credit score resources, financial wellness guides
